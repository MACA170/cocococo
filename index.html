Who Am I ?

About Me

Hi, I’m Sayfeddine Jalout, also known as MaCCreations, an 18-year-old graphic designer from Kenitra, Morocco, with 2 years of experience in crafting unique and eye-catching visuals. My work combines creativity, typography, branding, and layout design to bring ideas to life. I’m also passionate about incorporating cultural and artistic influences into my designs, creating pieces that resonate deeply with audiences.
